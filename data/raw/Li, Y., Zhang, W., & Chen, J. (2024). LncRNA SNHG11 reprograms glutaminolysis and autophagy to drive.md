# Li, Y., Zhang, W., & Chen, J. (2024). LncRNA SNHG11 reprograms glutaminolysis and autophagy to drive hepatic stellate cell activation in NASH. Biochemical Journal, 481(9), 1125–1142.

## Page /

“SNHG11 acts as a ceRNA to sponge miR-23b, releasing GLS1 mRNA from repression. Enhanced GLS1 elevates glutamate and α-KG levels, promoting histone demethylation at COL1A1 and CTGF promoters. Simultaneously, SNHG11 binds ATG7 3′-UTR, stabilizing ATG7 transcript, increasing autophagic flux. Elevated autophagy degrades SMAD7, reinforcing SMAD2/3 activation. These coordinated events drive HSC activation and fibrogenesis.”
