# Intellia Therapeutics. (2024). MAGNITUDE: Phase 3 study to evaluate NTLA-2001 in participants with transthyretin amyloidosis with cardiomyopathy (ATTR-CM). NHS Clinical Trials Registry.

## Page /

“By using the CRISPR/Cas 9 technology, NTLA-2001 will disable the TTR gene, but this does not involve the insertion of any permanent genetic material into the body. The purpose of this study is to learn about the effects of NTLA-2001 on ATTR-CM and see how safe and effective NTLA-2001 is for patients with ATTR-CM.”
