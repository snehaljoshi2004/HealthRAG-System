# Kotanidou, E. P., Tsinopoulou, V.-R., Serbis, A., Litou, E., & Galli-Tsinopoulou, A. (2021). Pseudohypoparathyroidism Type 1A with Normocalcaemia, due to the Novel c.389A>G (p.Tyr130Cys) Mutation in the GNAS Gene. Journal of Bone Metabolism, 28(1), 85–89.

## Page /

"Pseudohypoparathyroidism type 1A (PHP1A) is a rare disease caused by molecular defects in the maternally-inherited allele of the guanine nucleotide-binding protein, α-stimulating (GNAS) gene. The GNAS gene encodes the stimulatory G-protein α-subunit that regulates production of the second messenger cyclic adenosine monophosphate."
