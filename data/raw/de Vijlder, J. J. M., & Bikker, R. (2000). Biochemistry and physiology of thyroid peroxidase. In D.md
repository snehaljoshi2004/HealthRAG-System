# de Vijlder, J. J. M., & Bikker, R. (2000). Biochemistry and physiology of thyroid peroxidase. In D. P. Nauseef (Ed.), The Peroxidase Multigene Family of Enzymes (pp. 117–132). Springer.

## Page /

“Thyroglobulin is the protein in which iodine, bound to tyrosine residues, and thyroid hormone, formed from these iodinated tyrosine residues, is stored until needed. Iodination and coupling of iodo-tyrosine residues is catalyzed by the enzyme thyroid peroxidase (TPO) within the colloid of the thyroid follicle.”
