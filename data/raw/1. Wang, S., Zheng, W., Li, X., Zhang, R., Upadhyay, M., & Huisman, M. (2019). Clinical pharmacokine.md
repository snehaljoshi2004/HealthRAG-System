“Ivosidenib (AG‐120) is a selective, potent inhibitor of the mutant IDH1 protein. Preclinical studies showed that treatment with ivosidenib decreased intracellular 2‐hydroxyglutarate (2‐HG) levels in IDH1‐mutant cells in vitro and in xenograft models, translating into 2‐HG inhibition in human tumors.”
“AG‐120 (ivosidenib) effectively inhibits mutant IDH1, leading to a marked reduction in 2‐HG production and restoration of TET2 function, resulting in DNA demethylation and cellular differentiation in IDH1‐mutant glioma models.”
