# Peripheral Nervous System Involvement in Lyme Disease - PMC - PubMed Central. 

## Page nan

While idiopathic Bell's palsy is typically unilateral, bilateral facial nerve palsy is a highly characteristic, though not exclusive, manifestation of Lyme neuroborreliosis. Its presence in conjunction with other neurological or systemic symptoms should strongly prompt consideration and testing for Lyme disease, distinguishing it from most other causes of isolated facial palsy.
