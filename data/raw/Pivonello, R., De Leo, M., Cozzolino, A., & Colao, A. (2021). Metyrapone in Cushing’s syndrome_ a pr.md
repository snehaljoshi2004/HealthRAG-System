# Pivonello, R., De Leo, M., Cozzolino, A., & Colao, A. (2021). Metyrapone in Cushing’s syndrome: a profile of its use. Drugs & Therapy Perspectives, 37(3), 109–117.

"Metyrapone and its active metabolite, metyrapol, dose-dependently inhibit the two highly homologous isoforms of 11-hydroxylase, 11β-hydroxylase (CYP11B1) and 18-hydroxylase (CYP11B2), and consequently inhibit cortisol and aldosterone production."
