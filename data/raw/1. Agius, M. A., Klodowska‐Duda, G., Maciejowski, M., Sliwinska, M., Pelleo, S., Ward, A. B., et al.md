“Inebilizumab (MEDI‐551), an anti‐CD19 monoclonal antibody with enhanced antibody‐dependent cell‐mediated cytotoxicity, binds to and depletes CD19⁺ B cells. Objectives: To assess safety, tolerability, pharmacokinetics, pharmacodynamics, and immunogenicity of inebilizumab in adults with relapsing MS.” 
“Inebilizumab is an anti‐CD19 B‐cell–depleting antibody that, in a multicenter, double‐blind, randomized, placebo‐controlled phase 2/3 study, reduced risk of subsequent attacks and disability in NMOSD, illustrating its potent B cell depletion mechanism.”
