## Page Chapter 308: Gastrointestinal Bleeding

Radionuclide-labeled RBC scintigraphy can detect bleeding rates as low as 0.04 mL/min.

## Page Chapter: 93 – Neoplasms of the Gastrointestinal Tract

Neuroendocrine tumors of the gastrointestinal tract are managed by a multimodal approach that includes surgical resection for localized disease and medical therapies such as somatostatin analogs (e.g., octreotide), serotonin antagonists for carcinoid syndrome symptoms, and chemotherapy for advanced disease.
