# Romberg Test - StatPearls - NCBI Bookshelf

## Section

A positive Romberg sign indicates sensory ataxia, meaning the patient loses balance when their eyes are closed because they are unable to use visual input to compensate for a deficit in proprioception (the sense of position). This specifically suggests a lesion in the dorsal columns of the spinal cord or in the peripheral nerves that convey proprioceptive information
