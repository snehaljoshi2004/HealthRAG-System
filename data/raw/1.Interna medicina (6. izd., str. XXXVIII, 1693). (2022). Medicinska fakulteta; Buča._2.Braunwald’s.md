## Page 1. 234
2. pp. 1970 - 1974: Chapter 62 “Aortic Regurgitation"

1. Klinična slika. Težave se pojavijo, ko se mitralno ustje zoži na manj kot 2 cm². Glavni simptom je težka sapa pri naporu. Zaradi pljučne hipertenzije se lahko pojavi krvavo obarvan izpljunek, včasih tudi prave hemoptize. Pri popuščanju desnega prekata so bolniki utrujeni, slabo zmogljivi, otekajo v noge in v trebuh. Dilatacija levega preddvora pospešuje nastanek fibrilacije preddvorov. Ob pojavu fibrilacije preddvorov imajo bolniki tahiaritmijo, kar njihovo hemodinamično stanje še dodatno poslabša. English: Clinical presentation. Symptoms appear once the mitral orifice narrows to less than 2 cm². The main symptom is severe dyspnea on exertion. Pulmonary hypertension may lead to blood-tinged sputum and occasionally frank hemoptysis. In right ventricular failure, patients experience fatigue, poor exercise tolerance, and edema of the legs and abdomen. Left atrial dilation promotes the development of atrial fibrillation. When atrial fibrillation occurs, patients develop tachyarrhythmia, which further worsens their hemodynamic status.
