# Neuromyelitis Optica Spectrum Disorder (NMOSD) - StatPearls - NCBI Bookshelf.

## Section

Neuromyelitis optica (NMO), also known as Devic's disease, is a severe autoimmune astrocytopathy that primarily affects the central nervous system, with a predilection for the optic nerves and spinal cord. Its hallmark clinical manifestations are acute, severe optic neuritis (which can be bilateral and lead to blindness) and acute transverse myelitis (resulting in motor weakness like paraparesis, sensory loss with a distinct sensory level, and bladder/bowel dysfunction). The simultaneous or sequential occurrence of these severe attacks is characteristic of NMO.
