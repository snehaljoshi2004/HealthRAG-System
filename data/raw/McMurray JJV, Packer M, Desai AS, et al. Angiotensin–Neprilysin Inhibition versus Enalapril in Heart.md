# McMurray JJV, Packer M, Desai AS, et al. Angiotensin–Neprilysin Inhibition versus Enalapril in Heart Failure. N Engl J Med. 2014;371(11):993-1004. doi: 10.1056/NEJMoa1409077.

LCZ696 (sacubitril–valsartan) is a first-in-class angiotensin receptor–neprilysin inhibitor (ARNI) that combines the neprilysin inhibitor sacubitril and the angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB) valsartan in a single molecule.
